About Me

I’m a mechanical engineer who enjoys solving practical problems — the kind that demand clear thinking, smart design choices, and hands-on development.

Whether it’s a precision fixture, an automated station, or a subsystem for an autonomous robot, I like the moment when an idea becomes something real and reliable.

  • Think in systems — understand how mechanical, electrical and control layers interact
  • Build and test — because real insights happen on the shop floor, not only in CAD
  • Simplify — better reliability often comes from fewer and smarter parts
  • Design for real use — safety, robustness, tolerance, maintainability

Today, I work across mechanical design, automation, and systems engineering — creating solutions that perform consistently where it matters most: in production.
